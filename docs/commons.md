# Commons

The commons surface is the public-interest and community-oriented lane.

It is the closest outward-facing target surface to what people often read as a “consumer” story, but we should keep the language sharper than that.

## Why we prefer “commons” to “consumer”

“Consumer” compresses everything into a retail posture.

“Commons” better preserves the ideas we actually care about:

- shared public value
- community ownership or stewardship
- sovereign user control
- local-first participation
- educational and maker pathways

## Linux-site role

On the Linux site, the commons page should explain how the stack serves individuals and communities without trying to become a general-purpose mass-market marketing funnel.
